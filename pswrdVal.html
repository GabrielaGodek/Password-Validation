Password should contains:

  • Minimum 10 characters
    • The truth is that if you have more than 10 characters then better. Please do not limit yourself

    • Just Latin letters from your keyboard; the range is from a to z
  • Numbers
    • Just Latin numbers from your keyboard; the range is from 0 to 9

    • Special characters like: !, @, #, $, %, ^, &, *, +
